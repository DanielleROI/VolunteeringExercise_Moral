Who are we?

A community of volunteers for our neighbors, our friends and our environment.
We went through a very challenging year. At first it was a little fun to take a break, but it went on and on and hurting all sorts of ways in our lifestyle. Many citizens got into financial, mental and physical problems and so the venture arose. We took care of our neighbors, those who live behind the adjacent door and decided to take action. We actually started by cooking a meal or two for Friday night, continued with social gatherings, took care of warm clothing when winter came... and to be honest the volunteers always help us think of new ideas.
